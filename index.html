The problem

Perfect in the waist,
wrong everywhere else.

Standard sizing is built for an average body that no one actually has — so something always gives. The waist sits right but the hips pull; the hips fall beautifully but the waist gapes. Monequin begins from the opposite premise: your top and skirt are sized independently, so the set is cut to you — not to the mannequin.
